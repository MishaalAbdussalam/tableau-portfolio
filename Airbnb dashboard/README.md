# Airbnb Dashboard

This folder contains Airbnb-related analytics and visualizations for the Tableau portfolio.

## Overview
This dashboard analyzes Airbnb listing data across Seattle, Washington for 2016. It explores how pricing varies by bedroom count and geographic location, tracks how average prices evolved throughout the year, and provides a breakdown of listing volume by bedroom type. The goal is to give a clear picture of the Seattle Airbnb market, helping hosts, investors, or analysts understand where prices are highest, which listing types are most common, and how demand shifted across the year.

## Key Insights
- **Bedroom count is the strongest pricing driver** - Average prices scale consistently from $96 for one-bedroom listings up to $585 for six-bedroom properties, suggesting larger properties command a significant premium in the Seattle market.
- **Location plays a major role in pricing** - Zipcodes 98134 and 98101 record the highest average prices at over $200, while several outer zipcodes fall below $100, indicating that central and waterfront areas attract considerably higher rates.
- **Prices grew steadily through the first half of 2016 and plateaued** - Average weekly prices climbed sharply from January through July, reaching around $2M before leveling off through the rest of the year, pointing to strong seasonal demand in spring and summer months.
